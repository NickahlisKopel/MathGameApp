# MathGameApp Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 17, 2026

## Privacy Policy

Welcome to **MathGameApp** ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

Our goal is simple: collect only the information necessary to provide the features you choose to use.

---

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

* Email address
* Username
* Encrypted authentication credentials or authentication tokens
* Profile picture (if you choose to upload one)

If you use Apple Sign In or another supported authentication provider, we receive only the information that provider shares with us according to your privacy settings.

---

### Game Progress

To provide cloud saving and account synchronization, we may store:

* Total score
* Highest score
* Wins and losses
* Achievement progress
* Experience level
* Daily streak information
* Unlocked game modes
* Statistics related to gameplay
* User settings and preferences

This information allows your progress to remain available across multiple devices.

---

### Friends and Social Features

If you choose to use social features, we may store:

* Friends list
* Friend requests
* Public username
* Public profile information
* Leaderboard rankings

Only information necessary for these features is stored.

---

### Device Information

For stability and troubleshooting, we may automatically collect limited technical information such as:

* Device type
* Operating system version
* App version
* Anonymous crash reports
* Diagnostic information

This information helps improve the App.

---

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

* Create and manage your account
* Authenticate your identity
* Save game progress
* Synchronize your data across devices
* Display leaderboards
* Enable friend features
* Unlock achievements
* Maintain daily streaks
* Improve gameplay experience
* Fix bugs and technical issues
* Protect against cheating, fraud, and abuse
* Respond to customer support requests

We do **not** sell your personal information.

---

## Third-Party Services

MathGameApp relies on trusted third-party providers to operate certain services.

These providers may process limited information required for their services:

* Apple Sign In (when selected)
* MongoDB Atlas (secure cloud database)
* Render (application hosting)
* Expo services (notifications, updates, and development infrastructure, where applicable)

Each provider maintains its own privacy practices.

---

## Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

While no system can guarantee absolute security, we continuously work to improve the protection of user data.

---

## Data Retention

We retain your account information while your account remains active.

If you request deletion of your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable timeframe unless we are legally required to retain certain records.

---

## Children's Privacy

MathGameApp is intended for a general audience.

If we learn that personal information has been collected from a child in violation of applicable law, we will take reasonable steps to remove that information.

---

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

* Access your information
* Correct inaccurate information
* Delete your account
* Request a copy of your stored information
* Withdraw consent where applicable

To make a request, please contact us using the information below.
